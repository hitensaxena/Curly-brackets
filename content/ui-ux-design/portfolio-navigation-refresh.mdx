## Overview

The portfolio originally treated every artifact like a blog post, which made it
hard to signal why a journal entry, a music track, and an interactive work were
different from one another.

## Design Move

The main change was to split the information architecture at the route level and
make each section feel purpose-built without overcomplicating the shell.

## Result

Even before polishing the visual language, the site became easier to scan,
easier to extend, and easier to explain.
